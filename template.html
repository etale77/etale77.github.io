Stirling's Formula and the Distribution of Primes

11/21/2016

Stirling's Formula is a nice asymptotic formula that approximates the factorial. Explicitly it says that $$N! \approx \sqrt(2\pi N)\left(\frac{N}{e}\right)^N$$ While this is convenient for many applications in various fields, it is more than that. This formula actually encodes some fairly deep information about the distribution of primes that we'll investigate here.
